## Reach-First Strategy Note

For the next 2-3 weeks, prefer posts that help target users identify with `@wellwithinapp` as a calm fertility-awareness and natural-planning content source before leaning into app conversion.

Recommended mix:

- 60% identity/trust/reach content.
- 25% educational charting content.
- 10% product-light content.
- 5% direct app CTA.

Prioritize shares, saves, follows, and profile visits before App Store clicks. App CTAs should feel like a natural next step, not the point of every post.

## Queue Hygiene

Keep the active queue short and distinct. Before adding or choosing a draft, check the active queue, `Recently Published`, and `INSTAGRAM_EXPERIMENT_LOG.md`. If an idea repeats a published or queued post's audience, hook shape, metric, and visual system, merge it or justify why it should remain as a controlled test.
