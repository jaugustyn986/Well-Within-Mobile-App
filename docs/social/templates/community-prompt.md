# Template: Community Prompt

## Content Job

Community prompt.

## Best Used When

- The goal is comments, audience language, or account warmth.
- The next content direction needs better user wording.
- The account has posted several one-way educational posts and needs a more conversational rhythm.

## Target Metric

Primary: comments.  
Secondary: follows and profile visits.

## Source Patterns

- Simple reproductive-health questions from high-reach women's health accounts.
- Soft creator prompts that invite experience without asking for private details.

## Format Options

- Static prompt.
- Short Reel.
- Story question sticker if posting manually.

## Structure

1. Ask one clear, low-friction question.
2. Offer 3-5 examples to make answering easier.
3. Invite comments without pressure.
4. Follow up later by turning repeated language into drafts or memory updates.

## Visual System

Use a journal-led founder/voice post or creator note. Keep it human and simple; avoid overly polished app-ad layouts, boxed templates, or anything that asks for private details visually.

## Copy Patterns

- "What part of fertility charting felt confusing at first?"
- "What do you wish your cycle app explained more gently?"
- "Which charting word took the longest to understand?"

## Caption Pattern

Question -> examples -> comment CTA.

## Safety Notes

- Do not ask for sensitive cycle details.
- Do not answer individual medical or fertility interpretation questions in comments.
- Keep replies general, supportive, and educational.
- If comments reveal repeated confusion, log audience language in `INSTAGRAM_RESEARCH_LOG.md` and promote durable learning to `INSTAGRAM_MEMORY.md`.
