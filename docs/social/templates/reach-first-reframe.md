# Template: Reach-First Reframe

## Content Job

Reach-first identity or reframe.

## Best Used When

- The account needs more discovery, shares, follows, or profile visits.
- The post should help charting-curious users recognize themselves before hearing about the app.
- Metrics are too small to optimize conversion.

## Target Metric

Primary: reach and shares.  
Secondary: follows and profile visits.

## Source Patterns

- Calm creator-note reminders.
- Beginner self-identification prompts.
- Soft reframes that reduce pressure around learning fertility charting.

## Format Options

- Single feed image.
- 5-slide carousel.
- Short Reel with slow creator-note pacing.

## Structure

For a carousel:

1. Emotional or identity hook.
2. Lower the barrier.
3. Name one small charting action.
4. Reassure that clarity grows over time.
5. Save or send CTA.

For a single image:

- One strong line.
- Minimal supporting text.
- Save/follow CTA in caption.

## Visual System

Default to a journal-led creator note or founder/voice post: paper, calendar, notebook, phone-on-desk, morning light, or warm texture. Avoid app-ad polish, boxed card systems, and generic quote cards.

## Copy Patterns

- "You do not need to understand your whole cycle today."
- "One observation is still a start."
- "Fertility charting starts with what you notice today."
- "Your cycle is allowed to be something you learn, not something you guess."

## Safety Notes

- Do not imply incomplete charting gives settled interpretation.
- Do not promise calm, pregnancy, accuracy, or outcomes.
- Keep the app mention light or absent unless the CTA is profile visits.
